# Alliance Brawl
Alliance Quest Event

## Rules
- Defeat a standard opponent 25 PTS
- Defeat a Boss 75 PTS

## Rewards
- 100 PTS: 1xTier 3 **Class** ISO-8
- 200 PTS: 100 Loyalty 500 Battle Chips
- 300 PTS: 2500 Gold
- 700 PTS: 1x2-Star Crystal
- 1100 PTS: 1xPremium Crystal
